Egg Rolls

Home

Egg rolls are a crispy, savory Chinese appetizer filled with a mixture of vegetables and meat, wrapped in a thin pastry shell and fried to golden perfection.

Egg rolls are simple to make at home, combining seasoned ground pork or chicken with vegetables like cabbage, carrots, and mushrooms, all wrapped in a crunchy outer shell and fried until crispy. Perfect for dipping in soy sauce or sweet and sour sauce!

  • 1 lb ground pork or chicken
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 12 egg roll wrappers
  • Vegetable oil for frying
