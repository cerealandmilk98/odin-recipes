Crab Rangoon is a crispy fried appetizer filled with a creamy mixture of crab, cream cheese, and seasonings, offering a savory bite with a hint of sweetness.

To make Crab Rangoon, mix crab meat with cream cheese, garlic, and green onions, then wrap the filling in a wonton wrapper and fry until golden brown. These bite-sized treats are perfect for dipping in sweet and sour or soy sauce.

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 4 oz crab meat (or imitation crab)
  • 2 tablespoons green onions, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 12 wonton wrappers
  • Vegetable oil for frying
